Chapter II — Our Origin

We didn't set out
to sell furniture.
We set out to shape
how people live.

Monogram was born from a simple frustration — beautiful furniture that looked stunning in catalogues but felt wrong in real homes. We started small, sketching ideas on napkins, obsessing over joints, grain direction, and the way afternoon light falls on a velvet arm.

Today we outfit intimate bedrooms, sprawling hotel lobbies, co-working floors, and family dining rooms — all with the same obsessive attention to how things feel, not just how they look.
